Featured Work

Below is some of my featured work - while the site goes under some reconstruction.

Proportions of Transit: The Eliot Deviation Index - April 30, 2023
My presentation at TransitCon 2023, presenting on the research from me and the rest of the team behind the Eliot Deviation Index. (Video recording)

Multi-Family Zoning in Massachusetts - March 17, 2023
I wrote this paper for a class on land use planning and zoning, commenting and adding my own suggestions to Massachusetts' new MBTA Communities Law, a law designed to increase the housing supply near public transit in Massachusetts.

On Zoning: Pawtucket/Central Falls - January 25, 2023
A comprehensive look at the zoning surrounding the new Pawtucket/Central Falls train station, which opened to the public in January 2023.

The Hills of Worcester's Transit - June 9, 2022
An overview and critique of the operations and planning of the Worcester Regional Transit Authority, with short-, medium-, and long-term suggestions.

Calculating the right price for commuter rail - April 30, 2022 (CommonWealth Magazine)
My op-ed piece in CommonWealth Magazine, offering up a fairer way for true distance-based fares on the MBTA Commuter Rail.

Prioritzing Public Transit - January 28, 2022
A religious- and ethical-based argument for the expansion of public transit services presented to my high school class, along with a Q&A from my peers.

Fixing Fare Zones - January 24, 2022
The original proposal behind the overhaul of the MBTA Commuter Rail's distance-based fare zones.
